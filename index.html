Graphic designers decide the most effective way of getting a message across in print, electronic, and film media using a variety of methods, such as color, type, illustration, photography, animation and various print and layout techniques.They help to motivate, inspire, inform, and communicate ideas, concepts and brands by visually problem solving for a particular audience. Graphic designers use a variety of visual solutions and computer software, such as Adobe applications, to develop and create their design solutions.

      Graphic designers are instrumental in the creation of:

  • Layout and Design
  • Promotional display
  • Product packaging
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Logos and signage
  • Web sites and mobile
  • Motion graphics
